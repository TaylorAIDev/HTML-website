Our mission

With advances in technology, we have become globally connected, but locally isolated. At Konnect, our mission is to leverage the latest technology to not only bring back the connection we have lost in the modern world, but take it beyond what has ever been possible.

Social impact

Throughout human history, technology has taken what is scarce and made it abundant. Imagine a world where you could access human connection as easily as calling a ride. Here's our moonshot: we have a goal of helping 1 billion people make 1 new friend by 2030, creating an abundance of human connection.
